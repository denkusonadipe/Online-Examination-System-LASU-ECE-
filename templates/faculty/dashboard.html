Steps to create an exam
1 First step click on "New question"
2 On the new question page input a question, answer options A-D, and then under the "answer" input the correct answer for the question, and then input a mark for the question.
3 Repeat this process to add another question
4 Second step is to Click on the "new question paper" tab .
This shows you all questions previously submitted Select all questions needed for a particular exam And then type in the exam title on the QpaperTitle: And then click on submit
5 Third step is to click on "New main exam". Input your name in the space allocated for name .
6 Under total marks, input the mark you want for the exam.
7 Click on the question paper dropdown to select previously saved question paper
8 Choose the start and end time and then click submit
