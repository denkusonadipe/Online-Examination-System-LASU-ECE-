Learn More About Our system And Our Activities

The current method of taking examinations is quite hectic, and it’s a lengthy process of getting things done correctly. This necessitates a large number of staffs to organize students as well as a large amount of materials to conduct these examinations. Even after taking these exams, evaluating the answer scripts is a time-consuming process that is frequently ruined by human error during script marking and scoring.

This web-based examination system is developed using a combination of technologies such as; CSS, HTML, Javascript, Python and its capable of reducing proportion of workload on examination, grading and reviewing on the part of examiners. Thus, the system enables the release of examination results in record time and without error.
